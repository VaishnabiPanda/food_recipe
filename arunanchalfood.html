ARUNANCHAL PRADESH'S FOOD



Arunachal Pradesh cuisine reflects the rich cultural diversity of its many tribes and its lush, mountainous environment. The food is simple, organic, and largely influenced by local produce such as bamboo shoots, leafy greens, and meats like pork and chicken. A staple dish is thukpa, a noodle soup of Tibetan origin, while momos (dumplings) are also widely enjoyed. Bamboo shoot is a key ingredient in many preparations, adding a distinctive flavor. Fermented foods like pekey (fermented vegetables) and apong (a local rice beer) are common in traditional meals. Overall, Arunachali cuisine emphasizes natural flavors, minimal spices, and sustainable ingredients.
